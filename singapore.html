Singapore Tourism


Marina Bay Sands

Marina Bay Sands is an integrated resort fronting Marina Bay in Singapore, owned by the Las Vegas Sands corporation.

Sentosa Island

Sentosa Island is a popular island resort in Singapore, visited by some twenty million people a year.

Gardens by the Bay

Gardens by the Bay is a nature park spanning 101 hectares in the Central Region of Singapore.

Universal Studios Singapore

Universal Studios Singapore is a theme park located within Resorts World Sentosa on Sentosa Island, Singapore.

Chinatown

Chinatown is a subzone and ethnic enclave located within the Outram district in the Central Area of Singapore.
